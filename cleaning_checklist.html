The Ultimate Room-by-Room Cleaning Checklist

Your Guide to a Sparkling Home from Philly Home Pros

Kitchen Deep Clean

  • Wipe counters & stovetop
  • Clean inside & outside of microwave
  • Wipe down cabinet fronts
  • Clean sink and faucet
  • Clean appliance surfaces
  • Sweep and mop floors
  • Clean inside of refrigerator
  • Clean oven interior
  • Descale coffee maker
  • Clean baseboards & light fixtures

Bathroom Deep Clean

  • Scrub toilet inside and out
  • Clean shower/tub thoroughly
  • Wipe down sink and counter
  • Clean mirrors
  • Mop floors
  • Wash shower curtain/clean glass doors
  • Wipe down walls & baseboards
  • Clean light fixtures
  • Organize medicine cabinet
  • Empty and clean trash can

Living Room & Bedrooms

  • Dust all surfaces
  • Vacuum floors and furniture
  • Clean windows and mirrors
  • Wipe down electronics
  • Change and wash bedding
  • Vacuum under furniture
  • Dust artwork and decorations
  • Clean light fixtures & ceiling fans
  • Wipe down baseboards
  • Flip mattress (quarterly)

General House Tasks

  • Wash all windows (monthly)
  • Wipe down door handles & light switches
  • Clean air vents
  • Deep clean carpets (annually)
  • Organize closets and storage areas
