The Ultimate HVAC Maintenance Checklist

Your Complete Guide for Philadelphia Homes from Philly Home Pros

Monthly Tasks

  • Replace Air Filters: Check monthly, replace every 1-3 months depending on usage and pets.
  • Clear Vents and Registers: Remove furniture, rugs, or debris blocking airflow in all rooms.
  • Check Thermostat: Ensure it's reading temperature correctly and replace batteries if needed.
  • Listen for Unusual Noises: Note any grinding, squealing, or banging sounds for your technician.

Quarterly Tasks (Every 3 Months)

  • Clean Around Outdoor Unit: Remove leaves, debris, and vegetation within a 2-foot radius.
  • Check Ductwork: Look for visible damage, loose connections, or air leaks in accessible areas.
  • Test Carbon Monoxide Detectors: Press the test button and replace batteries annually.

Seasonal Tasks

Spring (March-May)

  • Schedule professional AC tune-up
  • Clean condensate drain line
  • Inspect and clean evaporator coils

Fall (Sept-Nov)

  • Schedule professional furnace/heating inspection
  • Clean or replace humidifier filters
  • Test heating system before cold weather arrives

When to Call a Professional Immediately

Don't wait if you notice: no heating or cooling, strange odors (gas, burning), loud or unusual noises, sudden high energy bills, or if your system is cycling on and off frequently.

Annual Professional Service

To ensure safety, efficiency, and longevity, we recommend a professional tune-up for both your heating and cooling systems every year. This includes a complete system inspection, cleaning, refrigerant level checks, electrical connection tightening, and more.
